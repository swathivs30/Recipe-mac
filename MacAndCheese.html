Mac and Cheese

Macaroni and cheese is a traditionally a casserole which is baked in the oven. The dish consists of macaroni elbow, cheddar sauce and cheese which is served hot. Here's a recipe for Mac and cheese served with salad. Bon Appetit!

Ingredients

  1. Elbow Macaroni - 8 Oz
  2. Shredded Cheddar cheese - 2cups
  3. Parmesan cheese - 1/2 cup
  4. Butter - 1/4 cup
  5. All purpose Flour - 3 tablespoons
  6. bread crumbs - 1/2 cup
  7. Paprika - 1/2 teaspoon

Directions

  • Step 1 : Cook the macaroni for about 6-7 mins.
  • Step 2 : Melt the butter in a sauce pan and then add the flour and mix to make a paste.
  • Step 3 : Add the cheese and mix until the sauce becomes thick. Pour this sauce over the macaroni ans stir well.
  • Step 4 : add breadcrumbs over the macaroni and cheese and srpinkle the dish with paprika.
  • Step 5 : Bake the casserole at 350 degrees for 35 minutes and serve with salad.
